Who am I?
My name is Matt Roberts and I'm a full-stack developer. I've been fascinated with computer hardware and programming since I was old enough to know what a computer was. I began my journey tinkering with my uncle's 8086 IBM PC over the summers while staying with my Grandma. Eventually my desire to control the hardware more than I already could took over and I began my programming adventure in C. This continued through college where I picked up C++ and then a few years later got a job doing C# web development. Since then I've developed professionally in Java, JS, TS, Node, and various SQL dialects.

In addition to programming I enjoy most everything related to technology and gadgets. Our house is outfitted a small army of Raspberry Pis for use as wiki servers, home theater, and emulation in addition to various other proprietary devices for home automation and entertainment. Outside of my digital life I enjoy being a weekend carpenter and woodworker. No matter what the project is I'm almost always pairing it with music. Some of my favorite genres include death and black metal, synthwave, and 80s pop.
